Administrator

Administrator is the FIFA President. He has the final authority to accept or reject a bid placed by a manager for an opposte team's player. There is only one Administrator in the whole application.

Managers

Managers manage the players of their respective teams. Managers can increase the size and strength of their teams by bidding for players of the opposite team. There is only one Manager per football team.

Players

Players form the football teams. When any opposite manager bids for a player, it depends on the player whether to accept the bid or not. If the player accepts the bid, he gets transferred to the team whose manager had bid for him.
